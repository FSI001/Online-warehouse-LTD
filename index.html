What are we?

We are an online warehouse where you can send packages and we wil store them for you this is the best service for you if you are an independent seller online in Dhaka, Bangladesh! Don't worry, if you are storing for personal needs, then this is also for you! For example, you sell stuff online, but you are lacking space, then you send all the extra items to us and we store them until you want them back! Here is an example for personal use, you have a very small house/apartment, and you have a lot of stuff, you can send it to us for the time being and get your stuff back as soon as you wish!



Who are our target customers?

Our main target customers are individuals who are running out of space and they want to store things for a cheap price!
